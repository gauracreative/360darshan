For a complete and immersive experience, a VR headset is required. However, if you are accessing the content using a PC or phone with a regular 2D browser, you may enjoy it more through the main page.

Tested with Meta Quest 2. It should work with Oculus Quest, Meta Quest Pro, Oculus Rift and Oculus Rift S, although not tested. An attempt has been made to support Vive headsets (based on coding documentation), but, again, not tested since I don't have those headsets. If you are using any of the other headsets (besides Quest 2) or any headsets at all please let me know how it works for you. I'll appreciate a feedback, and that should help me improve the app.

Opening it in VR: A Step-by-Step Guide

Update from July 4th, 2023: Discover Meta Quest App. You can bypass the following steps entirely!

  1. Wear your virtual reality (VR) headset.
  2. Access the browser on your headset, such as the Meta Quest Browser on Meta Quest 2.
  3. Navigate to the website vr.360darshan.com (you can bookmark it for easier future access).
  4. Within the browser, open a tab of your preference, such as Sri Vrindavan or Sri Govardhan (see above).
  5. Choose a specific holy place within the selected tab.
  6. Locate the "VR" button located at the bottom-right corner of the screen.
  7. Click on the "VR" button to initiate the virtual reality experience.
